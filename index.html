About me

A woman of contradictions. Light and bubbly, while also full of gravitas. Equally enjoys going out for drinks and dancing, and staying in for Netflix and knitting!


Interests

Photography
About 5 years ago, I treated myself to a DSLR (Nikon D5200, for those familiar with the industry). I've taken it on trips through national parks, to capture moments from other vacations, and to film and photograph my old a cappella groups!

Music
While I was forced into learning piano as a child, it formed the basis of all my knowledge and skill in music. Early on, I began to accompany myself while singing, and began playing guitar. Then it was composing and teaching a cappella music for multiple groups over 13 years (oh my, almost half my life I've done a cappella?!).
These days, I get my music fix as a part of the LSE choir!

Student Ambassador
I was selected to be a Student Ambassador for the Department of Management while at LSE, with my particular focus to be on blogging. Every day, I search for an experience that I can write about passionately, and that will resonate with incoming and current students.
