About me

A woman of paradoxes. Light and bubbly, also full of gravitas. I act my age and also like a grandma (I knit my own jumpers). And I throw a hell of a dinner party.

I decided to go back to school to join the evolution of ethics in marketing. Wouldn't it be wonderful to grow up in a world where media and advertising didn't drag down your self-esteem by telling you that you ought to look like the airbrused models they espouse?

Yeah. That'd be pretty awesome.
